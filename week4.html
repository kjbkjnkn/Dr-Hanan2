Brand Analysis Assignment

Assignment Overview

Group Presentation
Apply IMC and Consumer Behaviour frameworks to a real-world brand of your choice.

The goal is to move beyond simple description and provide strategic analysis of how a brand maintains message consistency.

Project Requirements

  • Select a local or international brand
  • Define the Target Market Persona
  • Complete a 4Ps Marketing Mix Audit
  • Map the Consumer Decision-Making Process
  • Present three key "Consumer Insights"

IMC Tools Audit

Evaluate the synergy between these specific communication channels:

Channel Evaluation Metric
Advertising Visual consistency across TV and Digital
Social Media Engagement style on TikTok vs Instagram
PR & Influencers Credibility of brand ambassadors

Evaluation Criteria

  • Conceptual Depth: Correct application of IMC theory.
  • Consistency Check: Evidence of unified brand messaging.
  • Presentation Quality: Visual clarity and professional delivery.
