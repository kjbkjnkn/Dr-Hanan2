Module 09

Digital Word of Mouth

Understanding e-WOM

Electronic Word of Mouth (e-WOM) is any positive or negative statement made by potential, actual, or former customers about a product or company, which is made available to a multitude of people via the Internet.

  • Speed: Spreads instantly across global networks.
  • Reach: One post can reach millions via viral mechanics.
  • Persistence: Online reviews stay visible for years.

Primary Forms of e-WOM

Online Reviews
Influencer Posts
Social Shares
Forum Debates

IMC & Reputation Management

While companies cannot "control" e-WOM, they can manage it through active engagement and consistent messaging.

Strategy: Proactive Engagement

Encouraging happy customers to review, using influencers to bridge trust gaps, and responding to negative feedback with professional solutions.
