Brand Positioning

What is Positioning?

Brand positioning is the act of designing a company’s offering and image to occupy a distinctive place in the mind of the target market.

"Positioning is not what you do to a product; positioning is what you do to the mind of the prospect." — Al Ries & Jack Trout

Visualizing the Market

Marketers use Perceptual Maps to identify how their brand is viewed relative to competitors across two key dimensions (e.g., Price vs. Quality).

This helps identify "white space" or gaps in the market where a brand can move to avoid direct competition.

Role of IMC

Positioning is only successful if it is consistently communicated. IMC tools work together to build this single, unified image.

  • Consistency builds brand recognition.
  • Inconsistency leads to "Brand Confusion."
  • IMC acts as the voice of the positioning strategy.
