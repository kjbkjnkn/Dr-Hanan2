Module 07

Communication Objectives

Defining Objectives

Communication objectives are the specific goals a brand intends to achieve through its IMC program. Unlike sales objectives, these focus on shifting consumer perceptions, attitudes, and awareness.

The AIDA Model

A hierarchical model representing the stages an individual goes through during the purchase process.

AWARENESS (Cognitive)
INTEREST (Affective)
DESIRE (Affective)
ACTION (Conative)
  • Awareness: Capturing attention in a crowded market.
  • Interest: Providing enough information to spark curiosity.
  • Desire: Moving from "I like it" to "I want it."
  • Action: The final push to purchase or sign up.

Strategic Role in IMC

  • Aligns all creative messaging under one goal.
  • Helps determine which IMC tools (PR, Ads, Promo) are needed.
  • Provides a benchmark for measuring campaign success.

Practical Application

Case Study: Smartphone Launch

Awareness: Global TV spots and Billboard Teasers.
Interest: Tech-influencer unboxing videos on YouTube.
Desire: Pre-order exclusive "Early Bird" bonuses.
Action: "Buy Now" links on social media stories.
