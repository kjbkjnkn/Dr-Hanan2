IMC & Situation Analysis

What is IMC?

Integrated Marketing Communications (IMC) ensures all marketing activities deliver a unified message.

Coordinates all promotional tools
Creates message consistency
Strengthens brand image
Improves customer trust

IMC Communication Tools

The core promotional mix components include:

Advertising
Public Relations (PR)
Sales Promotion
Personal Selling
Digital & Social Media

SWOT Analysis

A strategic tool used to evaluate internal and external factors affecting brand performance.

[Image of SWOT analysis matrix diagram]

Strengths

Internal brand advantages.

Weaknesses

Internal limitations.

Opportunities

External chances for growth.

Threats

External risks and challenges.

Importance of Analysis

Supports better decision-making
Helps identify target audience
Aligning objectives with reality
